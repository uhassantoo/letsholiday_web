About

Baku, the capital of Azerbaijan, is a dynamic city on the Caspian Sea. Known for its mix of modern skyscrapers like the Flame Towers and ancient sites such as the Old City (Icherisheher), Baku offers a rich blend of history, culture, and modernity. Visitors can enjoy beautiful parks, vibrant boulevards, and delicious Azerbaijani cuisine, making it a unique and exciting destination.

Airport Pickup

Baku City Tour

  • Visit to Highland Park-Alley of Martyrs , The National Assembly.

  • Visit to National Flag Square.
  • Visit to Baku Boulevard.
  • Tour starts with a Visit to Old City the Historical Core of Baku.

ABSHERON PENINSULA YANARDAG AND ATESGAH

  • Breakfast at Hotel.
  • Tour starts with Ateshgah (Fire Worshippers Temple) Based on Persian and Indian inscriptions.
  • Visit to "Fire Mountain" - "Yanardag" – Mountain on Absheron Peninsula, 25 km North of Baku.
  • Visit to The Heydar Aliyev Center - a 57,500 m2 building complex in Baku, Azerbaijan designed by Iraqi-British Architect Zaha Hadid.
  • Photo Shoot Stop at Heydar Aliyev Center.
  • Visit to Ganclik Shopping Mall – the biggest Mall in Baku.
  • Dinner at Local Azerbaijani Restaurant.

GOBUSTAN

  • Visit to Gobustan National Park and see ancient petroglyphs.
  • Visit Bibi Heybat Masjid.
  • 04:00 pm drop off at Hotel.
  • Lunch at Pakistani Restaurant.
  • Dinner Allowance 10 Mannat (Per Person).

AIRPORT TRANSFER FOR LAHORE

  • Breakfast at Hotel and Check Out.
  • Transfer to airport for flight departure to Lahore.
